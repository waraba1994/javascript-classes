About Us

Unlocking the Power of Data: Our Story in Data Analytics

Neldel works with small and large organizations/ companies to provide statistical consulting, analysis and reporting and data management. Our top priority is the quality of our work and ensuring we give the best statistical advice. Our team analyses data using excel, R, SPSS, SAS, Power BI and STATA.

  • History

    Our company has a rich history that spans over 2 years. We started as a small team of data enthusiasts who shared a vision of creating a platform that could analyze massive amounts of data in real-time.

  • Quality

    We believe that quality is key. Our primary focus is on delivering high-quality work to our clients, and we make sure that our team is always up-to-date with the latest analytics techniques

  • Vision

    We are excited about the future of data analytics and the role that our company will play in shaping it. Our Vision - innovating and improving our platform to meet the evolving needs of our clients.
